Tramiune (“Tramiune”) has developed this Privacy Policy to help you understand how Tramiune collects, uses, and discloses information Tramiune may collect from you and/or your device (computer, phone, or other mobile device), or that you may provide to Tramiune, while using Tramiune’s mobile applications and websites (collectively, the “Tramiune Services”).

By using the Tramiune Services, you agree to how Tramiune collects, uses, and discloses information that is collected from you. If you are under 18 years old, you acknowledge that your legal guardian has consented to this Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy is an integral part of the Terms of Use. If you or your legal guardian do not accept or understand this Privacy Policy, please do not use the Tramiune Services.

If you have questions or complaints regarding Tramiune’s Privacy Policy or practices, please contact Tramiune at support@tramiune.com.

1. Definitions

  • Financial Information: Payment and billing data (e.g., name, account info, billing address) used for billing only and protected by Tramiune.
  • Non-Personal Information: Information that does not identify a specific individual (e.g., demographics, usage data).
  • Personal Information: Identifiable information about an individual (e.g., name, email, device ID, phone number).

2. What Information Is Collected?

Information You Provide:

  • Email communications for newsletters, promotions, or events (with opt-out options).
  • Contact form submissions, including type of inquiry and purpose.
  • Geolocation data if enabled.
  • Push notifications for alerts and account info (can opt out via device settings).
  • Other interactions: search, contests, surveys, orders, communications.

Information Collected Automatically:

  • Tracking via IP, cookies, analytics, etc.
  • Device info (OS, device type, app version, Device ID).
  • Metadata for system monitoring, fraud prevention, and analytics.
  • Log files: IP, browser, ISP, referring/exit pages, date/time, clicks.
  • Mobile analytics: usage frequency, events, aggregated performance.
  • Local storage objects (HTML5, Flash) for preferences.

3. How Information Is Used, Shared, and Disclosed

Tramiune does not sell Personal Information without consent. Data may be used to improve services, provide support, communicate promotions, monitor system performance, or comply with legal obligations. Data sharing may occur for:

  • Legal reasons (court orders, law enforcement).
  • Business transfers (mergers, acquisitions, joint ventures).
  • Affiliated businesses or third-party service providers with proper safeguards.

Users can request deletion of personal data at any time by contacting customer support or via in-app support forms. Tramiune will handle requests in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
